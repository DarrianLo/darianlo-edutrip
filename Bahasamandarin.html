So for the homework i got while on my Jogjakarta edutrip for Mandarin Basic 2 was to interview the live in parents. There were several questions. 1. Where did i stay? Answer : Desa Pentingsari 2. How many people lived in the house? Answer : 6 3. How many kids did the parents in my live in have? Answer : 2 4. How old were they? Answer : 57 and 61 5. What did they work as? Answer : Farmers 6. What did they like to eat? Answer : Rice 7. What did they do in the morning? Answer : Work as a Farmer 8. What did you see there? What did you do? What did you learn? Answer: I saw numerous activities and what the Jogjakarta traditions were like. We helped clean the village, went on a lava tour on a jeep, and watched a dance. I leared so much new things such as how the coffee production is there and how to make tempe goreng and so much more.
